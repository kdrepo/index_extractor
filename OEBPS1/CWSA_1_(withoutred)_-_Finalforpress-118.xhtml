Record of Yoga, 26.12.1913 – Mahakali Tapas

“The Mahakali tapas has developed so far that now no defeat is taken as even temporarily final, except in temporary particulars, no nirananda is accepted, but at once converted wholly or partially into ananda, every suggestion of impossibility is rejected. Even the spirit of postponement of results is losing strength & the sraddha of immediate effect & rapidity is growing.” (361)
