Eternity and Infinity Possible in a Point

“This Eternal and Infinite is not only an Eternal of endless Time and Space. For its eternity can be realised not only in the endless progression of the years and aeons but in a single moment of Time, its infinity not only in the boundless extension of space to which we can assign no end or border, but in the most infinitesimal atom of an atom. But beyond the moment and the endless aeons the eternity of the Eternal is timeless and beyond the spatial Infinite and Infinitesimal the infinity of the Infinite is spaceless.” (178-79)
