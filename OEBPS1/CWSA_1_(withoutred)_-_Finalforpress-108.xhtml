The Main Cause of India’s Weakness

“It is my belief that the main cause of India’s weakness is not subjection, nor poverty, nor a lack of spirituality or religion, but a diminution of the power of thought, the spread of ignorance in the birthplace of knowledge. Everywhere I see an inability or unwillingness to think – incapacity of thought or “thought-phobia”.” (369-70)
