Publisher’s Note

This book contains selections from Volumes 1 to 22 of the Complete Works of Sri Aurobindo, published by Sri Aurobindo Ashram Trust, Pondicherry. The page references of the original sources along with the respective dates of those sources (wherever available) have been given after each selection.

Volume 9 of the CWSA contains Sri Aurobindo’s original Writings in Bengali and Sanskrit, English translation of which has not yet come into print, therefore the selections provided here have been taken from the “Bengali Writings” published in 1991 by Madanlal Himatsingka on behalf of All India Books, Pondicherry and the page references of the same have been given here.

All the titles of the selections, except the ones with an asterisk (*) to indicate the original titles, are from the Editor.

All the selections from the Complete Works of Sri Aurobindo appearing in this book are copyright of Sri Aurobindo Ashram Trust.
