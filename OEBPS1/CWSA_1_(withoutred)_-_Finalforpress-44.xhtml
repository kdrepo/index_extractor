Mr. Tilak

“No prominent man in India has suffered more for his country; none has taken his sacrifices and sufferings more quietly and as a matter of course.... Mr. Tilak’s name stands already for history as a nation-builder, one of the half-dozen greatest political personalities, memorable figures, representative men of the nation in this most critical period of India’s destinies, a name to be remembered gratefully so long as the country has pride in its past and hope for its future.” (656-57)
