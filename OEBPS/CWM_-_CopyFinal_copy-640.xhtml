Fits of Depression Caused by Vital Love

“H has had one of her usual disturbances. She wants me to tell you on her behalf: “I have given up hope, I have nothing in my fate except destruction.”

Yes, unhappily there is a vital love in her for you – it is that that has disturbed her balance, aroused in her this violent jealousy and brought upon her, as a consequence, all these fits of depression. But now no drastic or cutting way can lead out of the difficulty. It is by a growth of consciousness that this entanglement can be solved.” 21 September 1935 (119)
