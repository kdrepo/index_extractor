The Reaction of Ordinary People

“When you give yourself to the accomplishment of an unselfish aim, never expect ordinary people to praise and support you – on the contrary, they will always fight against you, hate and curse you.

But the Divine will be with you.” 17 September 1953 (281)

***

“Even if you were the only person in the whole world to give himself entirely and in all purity to the Divine – and thus being alone, misunderstood naturally by everyone upon earth –even if it were so, there is no reason why you should not do it.” (281)
