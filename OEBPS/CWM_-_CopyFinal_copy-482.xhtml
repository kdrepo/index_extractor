Sincerity

“Sincerity means to lift all the movements of the being to the level of the highest consciousness and realisation already attained.

Sincerity exacts the unification and harmonisation of the whole being in all its parts and movements around the central Divine Will.” 21 February 1930 (65)

***

“If earnestly you say to the Divine, “I want only Thee”, the Divine will arrange the circumstances in such a way that you are compelled to be sincere.” 8 June 1954 (66)

***

“Those who are sincere, I can help and turn easily towards the Divine. But where there is insincerity I can do very little.”
17 June 1954 (68)

***

“When you are sure that you have attained absolute sincerity, you may be certain that you have plunged into falsehood.” 7 July 1957 (69)
