Sri Aurobindo and the Mother

“Our Love is an eternal Truth. 7 April 1934 (32)

***

Without him, I exist not; without me, he is unmanifest.
6 May 1957 (32)

***

When in your heart and thought you will make no difference between Sri Aurobindo and me, when to think of Sri Aurobindo will be to think of me and to think of me will mean to think of Sri Aurobindo inevitably, when to see one will mean inevitably to see the other, like one and the same Person, – then you will know that you begin to be open to the supramental force and consciousness.” 4 March 1958 (32)
