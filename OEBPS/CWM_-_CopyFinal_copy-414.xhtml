The Teaching of “Spiritual Things”

“Spiritual things... They are taught history or spiritual things, they are taught science or spiritual things. That is the stupidity.

In history, the Spirit is there; in science, the Spirit is there – the Truth is everywhere. And what is needed is not to teach it in a false way, but to teach it in a true way. They cannot get that into their heads.” 5 April 1967 (401)
